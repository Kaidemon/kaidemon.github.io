## 📚 Features
NLP-Paper-Analyzer includes an 8-phase pipeline designed to streamline your research workflow:

1. **OCR (Optical Character Recognition):** Convert scanned documents into machine-readable text.
2. **Section Classification:** Automatically classify sections of academic papers.
3. **Grammar Correction:** Identify and correct grammatical errors in your text.
4. **Fact-Checking:** Validate the statements in your papers.
5. **Quality Scoring:** Rate the quality of your paper based on various predefined metrics.
6. **Deep Learning Models:** Leverage models like BERT and T5 for enhanced accuracy.
7. **User-Friendly Interface:** Designed for ease of use, even for non-technical users.
8. **Multi-Language Support:** Analyze papers in multiple languages seamlessly.
